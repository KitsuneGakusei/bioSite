Short Bio

Ralph is an adventurous and imaginative individual with a strong passion for immersive experiences. With a Bachelor's degree in Business Management, Ralph balances professional accomplishments with a dynamic personal life centered around travel, gaming, and theme parks.

Career and Goals

After earning his degree, Ralph began working in AV technology, installing systems for hospitals, government facilities, and even NASA. In the future, he hopes to find a career that keeps him closer to home and eventually teach abroad, exploring new cultures and sharing experiences through education.

Core Values

Ralph values curiosity, adventure, and creativity. His hobbies and lifestyle choices reflect these values in every way—from gaming and park visits to his professional aspirations.

🎨 Personal Style

Ralph’s favorite colors—orange, dark gray, black, and red—appear often in his wardrobe and merchandise. These colors reflect his bold, yet grounded personality and show up in many aspects of his daily life.

“Spend the rest of life having adventures and experiences in life together.”
